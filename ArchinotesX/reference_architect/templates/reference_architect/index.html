    By adopting an ArcOps strategy,

    You create a continuous workflow between architects, developers and system administrators aligning all the artifacts in your software.

    Keep your architecture and your app on the same track

    In a demanding business, software evolves, and its architecture should too!, ArchinotesX allows you to deploy your architecture. Test it, stress it, use it.

    Somethings just happen over and over again...

    ArchinotesX is oriented to the explicit scenario where there are multiple data sources and the business need is to constantly create and update microservices that will bring specific information from those sources.

    ArchinotesX is developed for a MSc thesis at Universidad de Los Andes by Dario Correal and Lorena Salamanca
